Making Melon Balls

What You'll Need

  • Melon
  • Knife
  • Cutting Board
  • Spoon
  • Melon Baller

Slicing the Melon

Melons can be a bit slippery, so be sure to have a firm grip. Never point the knife at yourself. Place the blade of the knife in the middle of the melon and begin to move it back and forth until you have split the melon in half.

Scooping Out Seeds

Use a large spoon to remove seeds and toss them into the compost bin.

Making Balls

Use melon baller to make melon balls
